Mercury

When Discovered:

Mercury has been known since ancient times and was observed by early civilizations like the Sumerians around 3,000 BCE. It is one of the five planets visible to the naked eye.

Size:

  • Diameter: Approximately 4,880 kilometers (3,032 miles).
  • It is the smallest planet in the solar system.

Atmosphere:

  • Mercury has a very thin atmosphere called an exosphere, composed of :
  • 1. Oxygen
  • 2. Sodium
  • 3. Hydrogen
  • 4. Helium
  • 5. Pottasium
  • This atmosphere is too thin to support weather or human life.

Distance from Sun and Earth:

  • From the Sun : Approximately 57.91 million kilometers (35.98 million miles)
  • From the Earth : On average, about 77 million kilometers (48 million miles)
