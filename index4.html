Mars

When Discovered:

Mars has been known since ancient times. Early civilizations, including the Babylonians and Egyptians, observed Mars due to its reddish appearance, often associating it with war and fire.

Size:

  • Diameter: 6,779 kilometers (4,212 miles)
  • Mars is the second-smallest planet in the solar system after Mercury.

Atmosphere:

  • Mars has a thin atmosphere composed primarily of :
  • 1. Carbon Dioxide (95%)
  • 2. Nitrogen (2.7%)
  • 3. Argon (1.6%)
  • 4. Traces of Oxygen and Water Vapor.
  • The thin atmosphere cannot support human life and provides minimal protection from radiation and meteoroids.

Distance from Sun and Earth:

  • From the Sun: Approximately 227.9 million kilometers (141.6 million miles).
  • From the Earth: The average distance is about 225 million kilometers (140 million miles), but this varies depending on the orbital positions of Mars and Earth.
